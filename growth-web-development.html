Our Web Development Process

1
Discovery & Planning (Week 1)

Understand your business goals, target audience, competitors, and project requirements. Define scope, features, timeline, and budget.

2
Design & Prototyping (Week 2-3)

Create wireframes, mockups, and interactive prototypes. Design UI/UX with your brand identity and get your approval before development.

3
Development & Coding (Week 4-8)

Build frontend, backend, database, and integrations. Follow agile methodology with weekly progress updates and milestone reviews.

4
Testing & Quality Assurance (Week 9)

Comprehensive testing: functionality, usability, responsiveness, cross-browser compatibility, security, and performance. Fix bugs and optimize.

5
Deployment & Launch (Week 10)

Deploy to production server, configure domain, SSL, and analytics. Conduct final checks and launch your website or application.

6
Ongoing Support & Maintenance

Post-launch support, monitoring, updates, backups, and enhancements. Monthly maintenance packages available for peace of mind.
