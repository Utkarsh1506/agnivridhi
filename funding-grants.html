Types of Grant Funding

Government Grants
  • Ministry-specific schemes
  • State government programs
  • Research & innovation grants
  • Sector-specific subsidies
CSR Funding
  • Corporate CSR programs
  • Foundation grants
  • Employee volunteering funds
  • Matching grants
International Grants
  • UN & bilateral agencies
  • International foundations
  • Global development funds
  • Climate & sustainability grants
Philanthropic Grants
  • Private family foundations
  • Community foundations
  • Impact investment grants
  • Crowdfunding platforms

Who Can Apply?

  • Registered NGOs: Section 8 companies, Trusts, Societies with valid registrations
  • Social Enterprises: Businesses with social/environmental impact focus
  • MSMEs: Companies in agriculture, technology, manufacturing, and innovation sectors
  • Research Institutions: Universities, labs, and R&D organizations
  • Startups: Early-stage companies in priority sectors
  • Individuals: Researchers, artists, and social innovators (for specific programs)

Key Benefits of Grant Funding

  • Non-Dilutive: No equity given up, full ownership retained
  • No Repayment: Unlike loans, grants don't need to be repaid
  • Credibility Boost: Grant awards enhance organizational reputation
  • Network Access: Connect with funders, partners, and mentors
  • Capacity Building: Many grants include training and technical support
  • Scale Impact: Funding enables expansion of successful programs

Our Grant Services

1. Grant Identification & Matching

  • Comprehensive database search across government, corporate, and international sources
  • Custom matching based on your mission, sector, and geography
  • Eligibility assessment and fit analysis
  • Grant calendar and deadline tracking

2. Proposal Writing & Development

  • Compelling narrative development highlighting impact
  • Detailed project plan with timelines and milestones
  • Realistic budgets aligned with funder requirements
  • Theory of change and logical framework
  • Monitoring & evaluation plan
  • Supporting documents and annexures

3. Compliance & Registration

  • 12A, 80G, FCRA, and CSR-1 registration support
  • Due diligence document preparation
  • Financial and governance policy documentation
  • Impact measurement framework setup

4. Post-Award Support

  • Grant agreement review and negotiation
  • Financial management and bookkeeping setup
  • Progress reporting and donor communication
  • Audit preparation and compliance monitoring
  • Impact documentation and storytelling

Application Process

  1. Initial Consultation: Understand your mission, needs, and grant readiness
  2. Grant Research: Identify 5-10 best-fit grant opportunities
  3. Proposal Development: Co-create compelling proposals with your team
  4. Submission: Submit applications with all required documents
  5. Follow-up: Track applications and respond to funder queries
  6. Award Management: Support implementation and reporting if successful

Success Factors

  • Clear problem statement and measurable impact goals
  • Strong organizational track record and governance
  • Realistic budget with clear cost-benefit analysis
  • Innovative approach with evidence of effectiveness
  • Sustainability plan beyond grant period
  • Community engagement and stakeholder buy-in
