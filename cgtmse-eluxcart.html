From Idea to Action: Elux Cart's Journey

The Challenge

Elux Cart began as a vision-to build a modern grocery retail chain that could serve communities across India. But turning that vision into reality required significant capital, regulatory compliance, and strategic guidance. Traditional bank loans demanded heavy collateral, which the founders didn't have.

Our Solution

Agnivridhi India stepped in to help Elux Cart navigate the CGTMSE (Credit Guarantee Fund Trust for Micro and Small Enterprises) loan process. We handled:

  • Complete documentation and business plan preparation
  • CGTMSE eligibility assessment and application
  • Bank coordination and follow-ups
  • Regulatory compliance setup (GST, FSSAI, Trade License)
  • Business structuring and financial projections
