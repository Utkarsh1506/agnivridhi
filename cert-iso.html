Our ISO Certification Process

Step 1: Gap Analysis & Readiness Assessment (2-4 weeks)

  • Review current processes against ISO standards
  • Identify gaps and non-conformities
  • Assess organizational readiness for certification
  • Provide detailed gap report with actionable recommendations

Step 2: Documentation & IMS Development (4-8 weeks)

  • Develop ISO-compliant Quality Manual
  • Create Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs)
  • Design Work Instructions and Forms
  • Establish Integrated Management System (IMS)
  • Define Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

Step 3: Implementation & Training (6-12 weeks)

  • Roll out new processes across departments
  • Train employees on ISO requirements and SOPs
  • Conduct awareness sessions for top management
  • Establish internal audit teams
  • Track and record implementation progress

Step 4: Internal Audit & Management Review (2-4 weeks)

  • Conduct internal audits to check compliance
  • Identify non-conformities and corrective actions
  • Management review of IMS performance
  • Close all findings before certification audit

Step 5: Certification Audit Coordination (2-4 weeks)

  • Stage 1: Document review by certification body
  • Stage 2: On-site audit of processes and compliance
  • Coordinate auditor visits and provide evidence
  • Address minor non-conformities if any
  • Receive ISO certification upon successful audit

Step 6: Post-Certification & Surveillance (Ongoing)

  • Annual surveillance audits (Year 1 & 2)
  • Recertification audit (every 3 years)
  • Continuous improvement and process optimization
  • Ongoing support and consulting

Who Needs ISO Certification?
