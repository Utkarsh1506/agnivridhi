Prime Minister's Employment Generation Programme (PMEGP)

PMEGP provides capital subsidy to establish new micro-enterprises and generate employment opportunities across India.

हिंदी सारांश: PMEGP नए सूक्ष्म उद्यम स्थापित करने के लिए परियोजना लागत का 15-35% तक सब्सिडी प्रदान करता है। यह योजना विनिर्माण और सेवा क्षेत्र दोनों के लिए उपलब्ध है।

What is PMEGP?

PMEGP is a central sector scheme administered by the Ministry of MSME. It provides margin money subsidy to new micro-enterprises in manufacturing, service, and business sectors through Khadi and Village Industries Commission (KVIC).

Eligibility Criteria

  • Age: Individual applicants above 18 years
  • Educational Qualification: Minimum 8th standard pass for projects above ₹10 lakh in manufacturing sector
  • Project Cost: Maximum ₹50 lakh for manufacturing and ₹20 lakh for service sector
  • Assistance per Family: Only one individual per family can avail PMEGP benefits
  • Prior Government Assistance: Should not have availed benefits under any other Government scheme
  • Self-employment: Existing units and those that have availed Government subsidies under other schemes are not eligible

Key Benefits

  • Substantial margin money subsidy (15-35% of project cost)
  • No collateral required for projects up to ₹10 lakh
  • Easy access to credit through KVIC network
  • Employment generation and self-employment opportunities
  • Technical training and capacity building support
  • Credit-linked subsidy with minimal documentation

Application Process

  1. Online Application: Submit application on PMEGP e-portal (www.kviconline.gov.in/pmegpeportal)
  2. Project Report: Prepare detailed project report with cost estimates, viability, and implementation plan
  3. Documentation: Submit identity proof, address proof, educational certificates, caste certificate (if applicable), and quotations
  4. Bank Loan Application: Apply for term loan with the recommended bank branch
  5. Verification: KVIC/implementing agency verifies application and forwards to bank
  6. Sanction: Bank sanctions loan and subsidy is released after project implementation

Required Documents

  • Aadhaar Card and PAN Card
  • Educational certificates
  • Caste certificate (for special categories)
  • Project Report with quotations
  • Address proof and bank account details
  • Passport size photographs
  • Any other documents as specified by implementing agency
