PMEGP Scheme

Prime Minister's Employment Generation Programme (PMEGP) provides capital subsidy for micro-enterprises and manufacturing enterprises.

Overview

PMEGP subsidizes project cost (typically 25â€“35%) to encourage new micro-enterprises. It is applicable to first-time entrepreneurs and certain categories of applicants.

Eligibility

  • Individuals above 18 years, existing self-employed persons (certain exclusions apply).
  • Not eligible if already availed benefits under earlier KVIC schemes.

Benefits

  • Project subsidy to reduce capital requirement.
  • Access to bank credit for remaining project cost.
