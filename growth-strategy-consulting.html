Our Strategy Consulting Services

1. Business Diagnostic & Assessment

  • SWOT Analysis: Identify strengths, weaknesses, opportunities, and threats
  • Business Model Canvas: Map value proposition, revenue streams, and cost structure
  • Financial Health Check: Analyze profitability, cash flow, and burn rate
  • Operational Efficiency Audit: Identify bottlenecks and process inefficiencies
  • Growth Readiness Score: Assess scalability and expansion potential

2. Market Research & Analysis

  • Market Size & TAM/SAM/SOM: Quantify addressable market opportunity
  • Customer Segmentation: Identify ideal customer profiles and personas
  • Demand Forecasting: Predict market trends and customer demand
  • Pricing Strategy: Competitive pricing analysis and optimization
  • Industry Trends: Monitor emerging technologies and disruptions
  • Regulatory & Compliance: Map legal requirements and entry barriers

3. Competitive Analysis

  • Competitor Mapping: Identify direct and indirect competitors
  • Benchmarking: Compare product features, pricing, and positioning
  • Porter's Five Forces: Analyze industry competitive dynamics
  • Competitive Advantage: Define your unique differentiation
  • Threat Assessment: Monitor new entrants and substitutes
  • Best Practices: Learn from industry leaders and case studies

4. Growth Roadmap Development

Short-Term (6-12 months)
  • Quick wins and revenue optimization
  • Product-market fit validation
  • Customer acquisition strategy
  • Operational efficiency improvements
Long-Term (1-3 years)
  • Market expansion and scaling strategy
  • New product/service development
  • Strategic partnerships & alliances
  • International market entry

5. Market Entry Strategy

  • Entry Mode Selection: Organic growth, partnerships, M&A, franchising
  • Go-to-Market Plan: Channels, distribution, and sales strategy
  • Localization Strategy: Adapt product/messaging for new markets
  • Risk Mitigation: Pilot testing and phased rollout approach
  • Budget & Investment: Capital requirement and ROI projections
  • Timeline & Milestones: Phased execution roadmap with KPIs

6. Product & Portfolio Strategy

  • Product Lifecycle Management: Launch, growth, maturity, decline strategies
  • Product Mix Optimization: Balance between SKUs and margins
  • Innovation Pipeline: R&D roadmap and new product development
  • Product Positioning: Messaging, branding, and differentiation
  • Value Engineering: Cost reduction without quality compromise

7. Business Model Innovation

  • Revenue Model Design: Subscription, freemium, marketplace, licensing
  • Cost Structure Optimization: Fixed vs variable cost mix
  • Channel Strategy: Direct sales, B2B, B2C, e-commerce, retail
  • Partnership Ecosystem: Strategic alliances and co-creation
  • Scalability Design: Build systems and processes for 10x growth

8. Digital Transformation Strategy

  • Technology Stack Assessment: Evaluate current tools and gaps
  • Process Automation: Identify workflows for automation (CRM, ERP)
  • Data Analytics: Build dashboards and data-driven decision culture
  • Customer Experience: Digital touchpoints and omnichannel strategy
  • Change Management: Employee training and adoption roadmap

Our Consulting Process

Phase 1: Discovery (Week 1-2)
  • Stakeholder interviews and workshops
  • Data collection and document review
  • Initial hypothesis and problem statement
Phase 2: Analysis (Week 3-4)
  • Market research and competitive analysis
  • Financial modeling and scenario planning
  • SWOT and feasibility assessment
Phase 3: Strategy Design (Week 5-6)
  • Develop strategic options and recommendations
  • Create detailed growth roadmap with milestones
  • Build financial projections and ROI models
Phase 4: Implementation Planning (Week 7-8)
  • Detailed action plan with timelines and owners
  • Resource allocation and budgeting
  • KPI dashboard and tracking mechanisms
Phase 5: Execution Support (Ongoing)
  • Monthly reviews and course corrections
  • Advisory support for critical decisions
  • Performance monitoring and optimization

Deliverables You Get

  • Executive Summary Report: Key findings and strategic recommendations
  • Market Research Report: Industry insights, competitive landscape, trends
  • Growth Roadmap: 3-year strategic plan with milestones and KPIs
  • Financial Model: Revenue projections, unit economics, break-even analysis
  • Implementation Plan: Detailed action items with timelines and responsibilities
  • KPI Dashboard: Metrics tracking template for ongoing monitoring
  • Presentation Deck: Board/investor-ready strategic plan presentation
