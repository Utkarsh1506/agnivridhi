Investor Readiness Criteria

We help you build these critical elements:

  • Minimum Viable Product (MVP): Functional prototype or beta version
  • Early Traction: User signups, pilot customers, revenue (even small)
  • Strong Team: Complementary skills, relevant experience, commitment
  • Clear Vision: Scalable business model with large addressable market
  • Metrics: KPIs that demonstrate growth potential
  • Legal Structure: Proper incorporation and cap table
