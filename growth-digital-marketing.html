  • Case Studies: Success stories and customer testimonials

5. Email Marketing

  • Email Campaign Strategy: Welcome series, promotional campaigns, re-engagement
  • List Building & Segmentation: Grow subscriber base and segment by behavior
  • Template Design: Mobile-responsive HTML email templates
  • Copywriting: Compelling subject lines and email content
  • Automation: Drip campaigns, cart abandonment, birthday emails
  • A/B Testing: Test subject lines, CTAs, and send times
  • Analytics: Track open rates, click-through rates, and conversions
  • Tools: Mailchimp, Sendinblue, HubSpot, ActiveCampaign

6. Conversion Rate Optimization (CRO)

  • Website Audit: Identify friction points and conversion barriers
  • A/B Testing: Test headlines, CTAs, forms, and page layouts
  • Heat Mapping: Analyze user behavior with Hotjar/Crazy Egg
  • Landing Page Optimization: Improve conversion rates on key pages
  • Funnel Analysis: Identify drop-off points in customer journey
  • UX Improvements: Streamline navigation and checkout process

7. Analytics & Reporting

  • Google Analytics Setup: Install GA4, configure goals and events
  • Google Search Console: Monitor search performance and indexing issues
  • Custom Dashboards: Real-time KPI tracking (traffic, leads, sales)
  • Monthly Reports: Detailed performance analysis with insights
  • Competitor Benchmarking: Compare performance against competitors
  • Attribution Modeling: Understand multi-channel customer journey
