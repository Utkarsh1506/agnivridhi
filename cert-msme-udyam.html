MSME / Udyam Registration

Registering as an MSME (Udyam) brings access to government schemes, priority in procurement and subsidies.

Benefits

  • Priority in government tenders and procurement.
  • Eligibility for subsidies and concessional loans.
  • Favorable treatment in various government programs.

Eligibility & Documents

Basic KYC of business owner, PAN, GST (if any), and bank account details are required. We prepare and file the Udyam registration on your behalf.

How to apply

  1. Collect KYC & business details.
  2. Complete online Udyam registration form.
  3. Download the certificate and Udyam registration number.
