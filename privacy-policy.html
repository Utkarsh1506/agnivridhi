Information we collect

We collect information you provide directly (for example, when you submit forms or contact us): name, email address, phone number, business details, and any documents you upload. We also collect technical and usage data automatically: IP address, browser and device information, pages visited, referrer, and analytics cookies.

Note: We do not sell your personal data. We may aggregate non-identifying information for analytical purposes.

How we use information

We use personal data to respond to enquiries, provide services, process applications for grants/funding, send transactional communications (acknowledgements, updates), and improve our website and services. We also use anonymized analytics to optimise site performance and user experience.

  • Service delivery: process applications, communicate with you, and fulfil contracted services.
  • Communications: send confirmations, updates and required notices.
  • Compliance & security: detect fraud, abuse, and to comply with legal obligations.

Security and retention

We implement industry-standard technical and organisational measures (TLS, access controls, periodic audits) to protect data. Access to personal data is limited to authorised employees and service providers under contract. We retain personal data only as long as necessary for the purposes described (typically contract term + statutory retention periods).

If you believe your data has been compromised, contact info@agnivridhiindia.com immediately.

Cookies & tracking

We use cookies and third-party analytics (e.g., Google Analytics) to understand site usage and improve functionality. You can control cookies through your browser settings. Essential cookies required for site operation may remain enabled.

Sharing & third parties

We may share data with trusted service providers (email, analytics, CRM, payment processors) who act on our behalf and under contract. We may disclose data to comply with legal obligations, or to protect rights and safety.

Your rights

Depending on applicable law, you may have the right to access, correct, restrict or delete your personal data, or to object to certain processing. To exercise these rights, contact info@agnivridhiindia.com. We will verify requests and respond within applicable timeframes.

International transfers

Data may be processed or stored in locations outside your country. When transferring data internationally we use standard contractual safeguards or other lawful mechanisms.

Contact & complaints

For privacy questions or to exercise your rights, contact: info@agnivridhiindia.com or write to Agnivridhi India, NX-ONE, TechZone 4, Greater Noida West, UP-201306.
