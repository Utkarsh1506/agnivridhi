CGTMSE & MUDRA Loans

Access collateral-free and low-cost credit for micro and small enterprises through government-backed schemes.

Overview

CGTMSE (Credit Guarantee Fund Trust for Micro and Small Enterprises) provides credit guarantee to lending institutions, enabling collateral-free loans. MUDRA offers loans under Pradhan Mantri MUDRA Yojana for micro-businesses under Shishu, Kishor and Tarun categories.

Eligibility

  • Micro and small enterprises operating in manufacturing, services or trading.
  • Business plan with viable cash flows and credit history (where applicable).
  • MUDRA: individual entrepreneurs, proprietors, small proprietorships.

Benefits

  • Collateral-free credit (CGTMSE-backed loans).
  • Low processing and interest concessions in some cases.
  • Access to working capital and term loans for growth.

Application Steps

  1. Prepare basic business documents and financials.
  2. Choose the right MUDRA category (Shishu/Kishor/Tarun) or apply for CGTMSE-backed loan via a bank/NBFC.
  3. Submit application and required documents to the lending institution.
  4. Coordinate with bank for sanction and disbursal; we can assist end-to-end.

Required Documents

  • Identity & address proof of promoter(s)
  • Business registration (if any) / Udyam registration
  • Bank statements / projected financials
  • Business plan and quotations (for term loans)
